OUR HOME FACILITIES

  • The orphanage provides free education to all children.
  • Children are enrolled in nearby schools for formal education.
  • >Evening study hours and tuition support are available.
  • Nutritious food is provided three times a day.
  • Clean drinking water is available at all times.
  • Regular medical check-ups are arranged for the children.
  • Emergency medical care is provided when needed.
  • Basic medicines and first-aid facilities are available.
  • Children receive proper clothing and shelter.
  • Hygiene and sanitation facilities are well maintained.
  • Emotional and psychological support is provided.
  • Extracurricular activities like sports and arts are encouraged.
  • Skill development and vocational training programs are available.
  • Safe and secure living conditions are ensured.
  • Scholarships and higher education guidance are provided for older children.
